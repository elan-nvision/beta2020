-THE DEVIL IN MY MIND-

A devil hugs me from the back.

Whispers in my ear.

Laughs like I can't hear.

Weighs down my head.

Plays with my mind

I try to ignore it although it swallows my time.

While this devil ignores me with a smirk.

And leaves me a little hurt but

It's time

I let this perish.

Hold up my head and

keep up my work.
