Wall


A wall.
It stands tall.
Resolute.
Blocking all.
A wall.
Crimson and grey.
Coloured with,
the soul of the prey.
Indifferent,
to war and peace.
Indifferent,
to pain and ease.
Resilient, unmoving.
Years pass by.
It stands strong.
Loyalties still lie.
Yet, slowly,
Ebbs and fades
Strength and power,
All evades.
Stains and splatters.
Crimson fades.
Mercy, murder
All evades.
Bricks and stones,
Weathered and frail.
Undying courage.
Refuse to fail.
It stands weak.
Determined to not fall.
It stands loyal.
Determined to stop all.

Another wall.
It stands tall.
Resolute.
Blocking all.
Another wall.
Crimson and grey
Coloured with,
The soul of the prey.
