Title - KALOPSIA

Warmth of the sun

Unadulterated and statuesque

Waking me up

Asking me to notice

I notice you coming back to consciousness

Blossoming into liveliness

Your favourite part of the day

Exquisite mornings of the summer season

This season reminds me of us

Our togetherness blossomed into friendship

And friendship blossomed into love

Comes the season of rain

Refreshing and scented breeze

Brings with it memories of our first mizzle

Dark and foggy clouds

Light drizzle , rumbling thunder

Asking me to notice

I notice you drenched to the skin

Droplets on your body

So beautiful

You and me ,we blossomed together into one

The rain witness to our promises

Now streets covered in crimson and yellow

Birds chirping , squirrels running

An air of serenity

Asking me to notice

I noticed that you left

Unexplained , Unjustified , Abruptly

Left me alone

Lying on the streets with other maples

Contributing to the magnificence of

The splendor autumn

The fall

The season of separation

Our journey together ,our love

A kalopsia

Appeared more beautiful than it was

May be an illusion

Which I wished to be true

Maybe a dream

Which I assumed to be real

Maybe be a delusion

Which I held onto

But it was right in front of us

Our fate

Inevitable , destined , inescapable
