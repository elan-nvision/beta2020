THE STARS
waking up next to you

with those sun rays falling on you

your eyes shined brighter than the stars 

and the galaxies I've seen in them

feeling your head on my chest

and my heart singing you to rest

my blood still pumps through you

the second you rest I will rest too

watching you going away from my sight

taking away my light

but, the air still smells like you and

every breath I take reminds me of you.....
