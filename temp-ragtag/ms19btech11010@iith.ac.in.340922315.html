#FeelingUnlucky 

I’m bruised oft-times. Trust me, from the very erstwhile age of mine as if I gladly welcome
those occurrences.  

The little-known people in the vicinity of a regular shop I visit, stare as if they’re expecting
my inbuilt-drama character on the street upon my grand entry .The next scene would
be me on the ground in a spur of the moment. Tragedy-O-tragedy!! 

Now that I’m taking-over those kind of situations, I’ve been speechless on encountering one
of my relatives who was dumbstruck on knowing how safe and sound I’m.  
How biased the situations are and the people who react !!
