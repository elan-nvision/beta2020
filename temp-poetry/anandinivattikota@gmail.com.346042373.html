Theme : Suffering 

Title : Tryst with destiny 

‘Tryst’-isn’t that “A Romantic Rendezvous” exclaimed French!

Yes,indeed a ron-day-voo between Destiny & Country ;

That night stroke the reunion of a tot & a freed mother;

Envisions of lads chasing kites and dwelling freely ,

High time to bid adieu to rigmarole of succumbness ,

Breaking the fetters of *bend-your-head* & shackles of darkness ,

Recollecting all the inevitably immortal stories of unsung heroes ,

Marching towards beam of courage & harmony ,

for ours is the land of valour & peace, my mate ;

Awakenings & instilling sense of optimism for future ,

Like a lost ship,Alas!Sailing towards winds of HOPE ;

Bright was the blazing torch of ZEAL that night ,

That night marked the end of a saga : Dawn break only meant infusion of aeration — refilled air charged with profuse life & victory. 

For it,was the midnight narration by beloved ‘Chachaji’ meant-a pact to have a life worth living. 

As the clock ticks,today, when the world is in deep slumber adjusting blankets of numerous issues ;

here I’m on the bed doing intense brain workout & ending this writing with query of HOW FAR HAVE WE COME? Inevitably,long way we came,but darl,long way to go as there is nothing to halt for. 


Jai Hind!
