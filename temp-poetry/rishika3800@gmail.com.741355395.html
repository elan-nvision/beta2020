Nostalgia

I was going home in the bus after a tiring day,

More of a shitty day,

I plugged in the earphones and scrolled through spotify.

It was the kind of day, where I was too lazy to pick the song.

I played a song in the playlist you made,

Flying whales,

"Water of chaos, Have invaded all space",

I felt alive,

Soaking in the sun;

The air blowing my hair around, 

A mischevious grin reached my eyes,

Everything felt beautiful,

Until the lollipop cut my tounge,

It left a sweet metallic taste,

It took me back to the time you left me,

I only had the dark metal playlist you made,

You named it buttercup with a cute red heart.

My bad, I kept forgetting back then too.

It's black metal not dark metal.
