Trepedation

In a world so wide,

I haven't a place to hide.

Everywhere I go,

No matter the flow.

The eyes I see,

Are always on me.

Even with no-one around,

It feels like I've been found.

What is this fear?

That drives me from here?

That makes my heartbeat faster,

Without any answer.

Infront of people with no sequel,

I stand unequal.
