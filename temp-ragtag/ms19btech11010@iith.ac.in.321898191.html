#FeelingUnlucky

 Swami was a coward , school going boy whose father puts him to adventurous tasks always. Once, his father read him the news of a boy applauded for saving the lives of farmers by fighting a tiger. Unluckily , Swami was ordered to sleep alone in the office. 

Reluctantly, he had to prove. Thoughts of untold mysteries of villagers death from the tree preoccupied Swami’s mind. As he was about to sleep , he woke up on seeing a distinct shadow from the window . He ran into his Grandma’s lap.
Father stared from the corner.

The rest's disgrace!
