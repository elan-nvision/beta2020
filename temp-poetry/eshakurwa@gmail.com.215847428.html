BLOOD

Its in the blood that we share,

The blood I don’t care.

I am out. Nothing left in there,

Out of home and everywhere.


Homes away, but blood’s still in here,

The blood I would love to smear,

Through the walls of haunted pain,

Through the nightmares of sheer disdain.


I’m out. Far and away.

Far from home and arrant dismay.

The blood’s turning green, and I don’t care,

Shame on the blood you and I share.
