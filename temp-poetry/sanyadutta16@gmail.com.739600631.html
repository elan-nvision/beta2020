Power- of a Mother

Ab bachpan itna suhana kyun lagta hai, jab bade hone ki khwahish thi hi us umar se
Shayad pakad liya tha maa ne haath shiddat se itni

Ke ab kismat bhi puche, ki kaha gayi vo lakeere chalte the hum jiske dum pe

Mushkile bhi bohot aayi hai, aur unse bada khudko samajhne wale log bhi

Par aankho mai nami to is baat ki chhaayi hai,

Ki inse bhidne ki taleem sikhane wale ko hi bhula dene se bada na hoga koi afsos bhi

Aa jati hai apni baato se dur karne adhurepan ko ye duniya bich-bich mai hazaro baar

Ab in matlabi begaano ko kaun samjhaye, 

Ki khamoshi se pura kardeti hai mujhe vo khila kar uske haatho ka khana bas ek hi baar

Khubiyon se apni waqif ho kar bhi, manzuri talasha karte hai gaero ki

Par yaara pucha nahi kabhi maa se apni, 

Ki kyun tarasha karti hai vo meri khamiyon ko bhi

Ghar ka matlab hota kya hai, ye pucho na zara hum jazbaati musafiro se

Eent ke saje aur maa ke saheje mai fark na samjha diya,

To band kardenge dena bhagwan ka darja aaj se use

Ab sharm nahi hai koi zindagi bhar tere pallu se bandhne mai

Ek tu hi to amar mishaal hai meri, uski bhi umar dhal jaye

To ghum jaenge kahi is andheri duniya ke sath ek hone mai

Aisa kya hai aakhir ek aam aurat mai, jo likh dalte hai panne tareef mai uski

Ab isme humara kya kasoor hai janab, 

Jab likh dala ho itihaas hi usne jhaansi thi jiski
