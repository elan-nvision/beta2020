ELAN & Nvision, the most anticipated festival of IlT Hyderabad, is back with a slew of grand events to captivate everyone once more. Since its inception, it has touched thousands, leaving behind wonderful memories in the hearts of everyone involved - participants and organisers alike.

The Events

  • Culti

    With the sole motive of entertaining the audience, CULTI promises to enthral the audience with an ensemble of out-of-this world performances and provide a stage for continuous and maximum visibility. Breakfree is the singature CULTI event where one can show off their individual talents or spellbind the crowd with their coordination.

  • Biggies

    Experience the heat of the dazzling array of shows that set the stage on fire. One of the major crowd pullers of the fest, BIGGIES is highlighted by events such as Campus Princess and Manthan, the race to find the band that really gets the crowd going or watch as the stage is converted into a war-zone as the bands sweat it out in a heated metal battle in Elan-E-Jung.

  • Techy

    This is the stage where the smartest minds of Hyderabad come to flex their technical know-how. The major attractions include Robo Soccer, the Quadcopter Challenge, a test of the speed, strength and agility of your drones and Drift King, an all-Terrain RC car race.

Campus Ambassador

The Campus Ambassador program provides you with an opportunity to be more closely associated with the fest than just another participant. A Campus Ambassador will be the first contact between ELAN and nVision 2020 and his/her college. The main responsibility of the CA is to encourage and ensure participation from your institutes in the events of the fest in ways you deem fit.
Interested individuals may register at the link provided below. The applicants will be shortlisted by our team and the selected Campus Ambassadors will be mailed.
