You looked beautiful when I first saw you,

and my heart just took me to another land. 

I was running in circles on top of the snow,

but it wasn't cold. 

I remember, we talked till late at night,

and none of us really wanted to leave.

But the magic happened when you passed me your hand,

we danced under the stars, and I remember you smiling.

For a sudden moment, everything else faded.

All I hear was music, and all I could see was,

us trying to follow each other's steps.

Yes, you almost tripped while doing the turn,

and we did laugh about it too.

All I remember now is how I felt about it 

when I looked at you.
