A leap in the dark world

  (suffering)

Strolling in the sun stroke
We work for our livelihood

Never bother how pale we are
Cause we’re so pale in our lives

Don’t mistake us being a dandy
Cause we don’t have enough

We dream of at least having a complete bowl
To clear our appetite

Our day goes on starving
although we work enough

There is no place where you can’t find us
In mines, industries, on roads ....

Unlike the other buddies,
We have no time to play , hop or rest

We wonder how a glutton who have much
Can’t be able to serve us little of he has

The night for us goes on unbeaten
As legs and hands swell out with pain

Although we won’t be able to live richer,
We just pass on having a dream of it

No time to play, no time to lay on the meadows
Cause we have to bend over our masters

We wonder how our future would be
We Pray genuine our imaginations could be
