- NUMB TO PAIN -

I got down.

Placed my feet on the ground and not even a while has gone.

I hurt myself.

I hurt my feet

I didn't mind to see it but

as I kept walking

it hurt,

hurt more

and my walk became less casual.

I go up the stairs .

I knew some eyes have noticed, asked me if I need care.

I couldn't be less bothered for care.

When I remembered

I should protect myself

as this is not the time to give up on myself.
