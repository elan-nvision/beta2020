I am afraid;

I am afraid that those eyes won't ever look at me with the love that I pour into them;

I am afraid to even steal a glance at those beautiful black pools for lest I glimpse the stranger that they see;

I am afraid that in that seeing right through me they readily reveal my insubstantiality; 

And cut off, therefore, the false things to which I've rooted myself,

And cast me into a Void,

Into a fall that never breaks...
