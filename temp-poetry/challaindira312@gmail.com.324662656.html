CAN YOU BE WITH ME 

Can you be with me,

                                 When I am alone,and staring blankly into nothing

Can you be with me,

                                 When I am happy to party around

Can you be with me,

                                  Wiping my tears when I am hurt

Can you be with me,

                                  To fight away all the obstacles in our lives

Can you be with me,

                                 Even if I do something to regret upon

Can you be with me,

                                 Just to tell me that you trust me, even if no one does

Can you be with me,

                                Like a true friend,that I have been longing for

Can you be with me ,

                              Love me as much as I love you

Can you be with me , 

Until the death seperates us , even in after life

Can you be with me,

Holding onto my soul

With our hands interwined together 

Standing at the end of the world

Gazing the night starts

Just hearing the sound of one's heart beat 

Falling in love forever.........
