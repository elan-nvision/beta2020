Suffering

Title - I won't kill myself

6:18 am , still 12 minutes for the alarm to ring

But anxiety doesn't need an alarm

It wakes up and automatically sets in

My anxiety is my shadow

Always by my side

Little and lighter at noon

Grows bigger and darker

By the time the sun sets

And finally

Engulfs me at night

Puts me in a race that wouldn't end

Creates a panic that wouldn't stop

Ignites a fire that burns me down

At 6:30 ,finally I wake up

Hoping anxiety goes back to sleep soon

But will it ?

No , it won't !

I see the fan hanging on the ceiling

And think

What if I just hang myself ?

I go to the terrace to get some fresh air

And think

What if I just jump ?

I come back , enter the bathroom , go inside the bathtub

And think

What if I just drown myself in ?

Maybe I am a jigsaw puzzle now

Waiting for pieces to come together and make sense

But I wouldn't be a jigsaw puzzle always

Everything will fall into place

I'll be whole and beautiful

Life is a book

And I don't want to leave this book unfinished

Maybe today I am stuck in the Tragedy chapter

But I wouldn't be here always

I hope the chapter of Tranquility comes soon

I won't kill myself

I will kill all the self depreciating thoughts

Because sometimes

You don't need a superhero to come and save you

Sometimes

You need to be that superhero
