SWEETHEART 

I think Stephen Hawking is
true. 

“Time can be infinite.” 

The first time you walked
into the class, the first time our eyes met as we grazed into each other, the
first time we shared a smile, felt like the universe has paused for us to enjoy
the moment. 

It’s the moment I felt you’re
gonna be my SWEETHEART… 

Entire class thought it was
embarrassing for us to shout the same wrong answer in the class. Little did
they know that it’s gonna be one of my most memorable moments. Well, it’s bound
to happen, as our minds connected as well as our hearts. 

I was on my nerve to ask you
out on a date. But when you said yes, the amount of adrenaline released in me
was…., infinite. 

Every single minute , hour ,
day I spent with you, thinking what we could become, made me fall for you more. 

The time, when we used to
walk on that empty night street, holding each other’s hands, talking nothing,
but still understand each other, is the time I knew I wanted this for the rest
of my life. 

But destiny had other plans.
Sure, it was hard when we wanted different things in life, as it gave a hint
that nothing lasts forever. 

Now I look back and see
nothing but our matching footsteps leaving an impression of this incredible
journey, till we drifted apart. 

Many might come and leave,
but I’ll always remember you as my first wondrous 

SWEETHEART…..
