Fly

They cut her wings,
And tell her to fly.
What should she do,
If not cry?
And they swim with joy,
In her stream of tears,
Soon to be a stream
Of blood, she fears.
They push her farther and farther,
Hoping she'll fly.
When she is unable to,
They say she doesn't try.
And they keep forgetting,
How they broke her wings.
When she reminds them,
It's warded off as "little things"
They try to push her off the cliff,
Praying she'll learn,
But she knows, SHE KNOWS,
Yet it'll never be her turn.
And they keep cursing her,
For being "absurd",
Her voice, it's lost,
Never heard.
