From being a whiff of cool breeze

Navigating your way through hundreds of branches

On a hot humid summer

Your mere touch , euphoric

To this violent storm you have become

Destroying everything that comes your way

A havoc which battered me too

In the world we knitted together

I was a careless free bird

Piercing my way through endless blue sky

When you cut my wings

And caged me

You got lost in a world , which now i can never be a part of

You were once the fragrance of the first rain

Scent that evoked nostalgia

An intoxication that overpowered me

But eventually you too ,

Like the fragrance, faded away

And soon the inebriated state you left me in

Will fade too

From loud catastrophic deafening noises

To deafening silences

Where it's been weeks I heard my own voice

For the last time

I shouted your name

But it got lost in a thousand other voices that called you
