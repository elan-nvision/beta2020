Let even our vellus hairs groom each other only in the thousandth of our lives;

Yet let us move towards each other ever so slowly;

Like the calm breeze that makes the solitary leaf of a wild vine dance in anticipation of the the rain that comes to quench its thirst...

And yet unlike it for being a millonth slower.

And in that movement, be still.

Let our love move us ever so slowly

Like Gravity;

humble to a fault,

fleeting in the closest of scrutiny,

Yet.

the Mover of gargantuans
