You and I

Even the sunlight abandons the sun in the dark..
So the moonlight let go the moon as dawn comes by..
You are to me, like what waves are to ocean..
Always besides, be the tides low or high…

You are mistaken my love, she said..
How can I part from you..
Even my name is incomplete without yours..
So am I incomplete, without you…

I love the words, each one of you say,
(explain me how mistaken am I)
Even the stars defy the sky…
How can they be compared to the bond we share…?
How can they be compared to you and I..?

Our love is not for the materialistic world..
Or how the people see, dear..
Stars remain with sky along..
But to the normal eyes, they don’t appear..

Light is never away from the Luna and Sun..
They always carry the spark.
They are gone away so far
For those who cherish the dark

Our love is pious and true..
So shed all your fear,
Hold me tight, hold me close..
And for eternity, love me dear…
