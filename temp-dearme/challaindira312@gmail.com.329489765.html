To 

My
22-year-old  

Dear me, 

Hello, I hope you are doing good. 

From where should I start, first remember one thing I LOVE YOU no matter what you do and
where you end up. You are always good enough. Never regret the choices you
make.do not cry on your mistakes, we are humans after all we tend to make a lot
of mistakes. Learn to be happy with yourself.do things which make you feel
euphoric, even if it is big or small. Grasp every opportunity that comes your
way, that make you happy so that you wont regret of not taking it up later.
Enjoy little things in life. don’t set long time goals, it’s okay to not to
have a goal as long as you are happy, the world will automatically recognize
you. You can give up on anything in life when you feel exhausted but don’t give
up on yourself. Remember this past me is always rooting for you and I will
always believe you and the choices you make. Be independent and don’t let your
decisions be influenced by others be it your mom, dad, sister or anyone u love.
You can be yourself and people still will find a problem with it. So just be
yourself even more. Irritate their demon’s girl! Whenever you feel low, when
you that the world is against you, when you feel that you are walking on a road
alone without any destination, stop for sometime and read this letter. You are
strength to yourself, don’t doubt yourself even for one moment in life. I trust
you. And don’t forget to travel around the world clicking millions of selfies. 

Let’s get it girl  

Yours lovingly 

Past self 

Challa sai charitha
