Title -Be yourself

 Be yourself, 

You have one life to live, 

Compromise and adjust are good words, 

But don't let yourself fade away in the mid process, 

You are here to survive for others but most importantly for yourself,

 Don't let the inner demons and the chronching voices of people affect you, 

Don't die with regrets;

 die with memories instead, 

You have one life, Be yourself
