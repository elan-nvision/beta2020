Autumn

Leaves mirror skies,
Reflecting yellow.
Withering.

Leaves fall,
On the ground
Slowly.
Like the sky,
On my shoulders.
Crushed, both,
the leaves,
and my shoulders
by a stampede.
Of love.
Of war.
Remanants wander.

Leaves raked.
Pushed aside.
Try to fly away,
Held down.
Like the emotions,
Of my heart.
I feel no more.
I am no more.
And now,
I want to be no more.

Leaves burnt.
Black.
Like the ashes,
Of my soul.
Smoke surrounding.
Burnt leaves,
Pay no heed.
But me,
I may have no soul.
But I have a body.
Smoke surrounding.
I choke.
