The Light

It goes down the road and 

then up the hill, you don't know

where you want to go for real,

but you change your path through

the way, without knowing, where

 the path you choose will fade away.

Still, you carry the journey 

through the way, cause you

believed to go through the way

knowing that it might not be safe

or having any idea of destiny it takes.

Resting over the milestone on

the night so cold that it could have 

chilled without the warmth of the faith 

and the way too dark that I would 

have been lost without the light, 

 and your touch and feel.

We reach the end of the path of dark

and had a fall there, neither swiftly

nor willingly but with the darkness was endured.

It was your light, which was bright of all, 

and we never realized that we had it all,

not from that moment, but the start,

 or maybe when you choose me for the worst days of yours. 

With dangling feet and rushing hands, we wnt that far

when hope was falling, you showed it all.

Now the light is brightest of all,

 with the darkness of zillion voids of stars.

It was the light,

with which we traveled till the fall,

which became more beautiful in the end.

It traveled in time but remained. 

It reaches infinity and eternity, and beyond, the wise man cannot tell its end,

or maybe it is just beyond the world in existence.
