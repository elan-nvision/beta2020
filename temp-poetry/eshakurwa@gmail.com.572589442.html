THOUSAND MILES

As I look back to who we were,

As I struggle to remember,

All these years, all those thousand miles,

Can only bring up smiles.


You remember back in fifth grade,

Those funny games we played,

The fun we had in parade,

The comics we bade,

The cookies we made,

Those paints we sprayed….

Can we go back a decade?

Can we please go back to those careless smiles?

Can we for once , go back those thousand miles?


I still reminisce math class,

Where we only played XO and SOS.

And when the teacher came to our table,

I remember how we toiled to keep ourselves stable.


You remember the time we coloured our tongues blue?

Our dreams of having the same Dark Mark tattoo,

How we once refilled lotion bottle with shampoo,

Those essays we had to redo,

Because we wrote lyrics instead of book review,

Thinking no one’s gonna check, its all untrue…


I wish we lived that once again,

I wish we regrew,

For once, I wish we started again, started anew. 

I received your letter yesterday,

And it bought with it all smiles,

Funny how we never gave up on that old practice,

Funny how that letter still bonds us over those thousand miles.
