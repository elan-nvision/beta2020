The past decade - golden age or garbage age?

The last decade has brought a lot of changes to the world and transformed our lives altogether. Small changes that have contributed to a big impact.

The progress in science and technology is the factor that has played a significant role in changing the way everything works now. The modern world is more connected with the help of the internet. Almost all tedious and time consuming processes such as money transfer, booking tickets, ordering food is done in a matter of minutes these days. Technology has its influence even in the world of politics. Politicians have better interaction with people through social media. People share and post theirs views on the internet, thus they exercise their right to expression much more.

The whole world is now more connected. Culture is evolving with time and here is an exchange of culture and ideas between countries. Limited Memory Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning has become a reality.

Back then, black hole was just in theory but it's captured image confirmed its existence this year. That indicates how much development the past decade has seen.

However on the flip side, there are several repercussions as well. The modern world is slowly turning into a dystopian society where humans are becoming more and more mechanical and machines are getting smarter and more human. Children used to play with toys back then but now even they are found with smartphones and other gadgets. We have become more dependent on gadgets.. Smart phones have more or less become an extension of each individual as they spent a significant amount of their time with it."Nomophobia" is now an actual thing. There is a loss of emotional connection between people. Social media has gained more popularity and the number of people addicted to it has gone up exponentially. As a consequence, attention span of humans have become much lower.

Back in those days, people used to interact with others and cherish important moments. However, nowadays people just click selfies and post it online to get likes and validate themselves. Pollution levels have gone up and the balance in the ecosystem is more disturbed now than it used to be.

Overall, the present age that we live in is definitely not like the one we used to ten years ago. Afterall, change is the only thing that remains constant. Although it is true that we are living more comfortably now, one can't deny that they don't miss the good old days.
