I open at close

It’s nor your fault neither it’s mine,
For I’m the demon, and you divine.
So let evaporate the worldly desire,
Cause you’re ice and I’m fire.

Let me fly away, breaking your chains,
Besides you, my shadow remains.
Let me ride the dragon and fly high,
With this demon away, your worries shall die.

For the battles and wars, don’t you fear,
Cause I’ll be beside you, always my dear.
But not the one, the world will see,
Not the flesh and blood, but essence of me will always be.

With the end of this tale, a new shall begin
Written by Gods, and which the stars shall sing.
And thus the last line of my rhyme goes,
You close at open, I open at close…
