Eclipse

Theme: Suffering

Come sit down, I have a confession to make

Often I peek into your room in the dark

And I see you lost in the shadows

Moving like a ghost in the night

My daughter, my baby, my whole heart.

I have so much to say to you

But the words, they refuse to leave my mouth

And remain wrapped around my tongue, unspoken.

I want to tell you that I know,

I’ve seen it all too

You think you’re broken beyond repair

But my darling, that’s not true

You’ll pick yourself up before you fall apart

Just like I did, too

I know this feels like the end of the world

But you have your whole life ahead of you,

My child, if only you knew.

You can keep on hiding in your room

Like a wilted flower, gone out of bloom

But please don’t give up, not now, not so soon

For you were meant to shine on like the moon.

These scars you wear like second skin

Smile and cover them up, for just one day

The tears that feel like they’d drown you

Smile and wipe them off, for just one day

The heartbreak threatening to choke you

Smile and let it go, for just one day

Just one day.

Can you be a hero for just one day?
