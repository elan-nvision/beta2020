#IndiaIncredible

is the best place. Cost of living is low & may be of life too. Men can cozily
take up retaliation by murdering openly . Overloaded auto-rikshaws , high-pitched
voices of vendors right in the morning ! Free to spit anywhere and assume open spaces as
public toilets. Produce fake ids, proofs on bribing, perform stunts on bike and barely get noticed.
Cool ! isn't it ? Impressive one-day plays of swachhta , yoga and what not ?  

India is truly incredible!
