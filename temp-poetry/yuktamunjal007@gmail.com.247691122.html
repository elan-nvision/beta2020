Suffering 

Stuck in a room somewhere

With No doors and no windows

No way to get out

Petrified and terror stricken

I am glued to the floor

Just Sitting there and waiting . Waiting for what ?

I don't know , for answers maybe

I am a mess , A crumpled sheet of paper

A maze ,a mystery

Screaming at the top of my lungs But no voice coming out .

Felt like crying but couldn't shed tears

Why is nobody listening to me ?

Why is nobody responding ?

Am I dumb ? Or are they deaf ?

Lying supine in my bed

Replaying the same incident

Again and again and again

An endless loop , A constant hassle

Didn't do anything , just lying there motionless

But there is something that makes me feel

I fought a battle ,And lost it

Lost everything

A stranger within me , Holding me hostage

He my master and I, his puppet

It's his home and he is not going back

And I am the traveller with no destination

Just walking endlessly

Lost somewhere , chasing something

The mirage of happiness

But the moment I touch it ,It vanishes

Gone , just like that

The mirror hanging there , my foe

I shy away from it

Reminds me of how I changed , how I used to be , how I want to go back

But something pushing me down ,Stopping me

What is it ?

No name , no identify .But something

A feeling

A feeling of constant fear

As if someone holding a gun against my head

As if standing on a cliff and looking down

Just a matter of time and I'll be gone

And I'll be fine

At Least it will be over for once and for all
