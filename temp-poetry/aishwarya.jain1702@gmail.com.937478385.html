Grey 

Smoking dreams,
Lost hope.
'Round the neck,
A bloody rope.
Grey words,
Aimlessly hover.
Grey haters,
Only grey lovers.
Faint movements,
Weakening pulse.
Grey time.
The clock mulls.
Smoking dreams,
Lost hope.
'Round the neck,
A bloody rope.
Grey warriors,
Bloody slain.
Grey hearts,
Embrace pain.
Grey hearts,
Bound with chains.
In grey skies,
The moon wanes.
Smoking dreams,
Lost hope.
'Round the neck,
A bloody rope.
Grey days,
Go on and on.
Back and forth,
Dusk till dawn.
Grey emotions,
Empty head.
Grey lives,
Stagnant, dead.
Smoking dreams,
Lost hope.
'Round the neck,
A bloody rope.
In a grey world,
Woes tout.
Only grey souls
Wander about.
