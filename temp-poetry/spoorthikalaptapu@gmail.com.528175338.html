WHEN IT WON'T COME TO BE 

Its just not enough

I do seek to be tough

Resign, I will, when I have given all too much

Dreary, the becoming of what I hold dear as such

To have to devour a mountain more than I can fathom

Is to return to an inglorious anthem

What I have to tell myself then, 

"When it wont come to be 

Let it be".
