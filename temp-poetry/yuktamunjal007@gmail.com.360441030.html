Nostalgia

Sooner or later , nostalgia comes knocking on the door

Makes itself comfortable ,

When I stare at all the failed attempts I made

To avoid the empty feeling called homesickness

I part of me still wants to go back

Go back to the time when I could call that place home

To contribute to all the smiles and laughter that I always wished to be a part of

Because even after all these years

Celebrations here, do not feel like celebrations

A mere formality maybe

Which i always wait to get over

I see many faces greeting one another

But still long for the familiar one's back there

No matter how many gift wraps I open

I still yearn for those home sweets

Because no matter how broken the house maybe

Some of the pieces always remain with you

Wanting you to go back someday

To the place called home
