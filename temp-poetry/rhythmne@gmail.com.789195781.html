A HAPPY ENDING

In this timeless, fleeting moment

The clouds of ambiguity subside

As the rays of the burning world

Pierce into my grey heart

It had long been jealous of the mind,

Who had not felt as much pain

For it had always been this grey.

Taking control, this mighty being

Banished all the fears. The fire ceased to burn,

Mountains appeared no more than heaps of sand

And the oceans no longer had enough water.

The only one that seemed to be happy

Was the soul which would finally be set free.

Free from the shackles of the mind and the

Cage that would crumble back into dust.

It was over in the snap of a second. Peaceful silence prevailed.

The childhood friends lay side by side in harmony.

Do not cry, petty humans! It was meant to be.

Instead, look up at the caged bird soaring the skies

And witness the world undergo the great mending

Smile and rise for it is indeed a happy ending.
