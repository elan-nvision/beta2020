THE CHALLENGE

I challenge life to become a hero

I challenge death to survive

I challenge the hardships to be stronger

I challenge the shy to be more courageous

I challenge everything to be a myth

I challenge myself to be better

I challenge the darkness to enlighten peace

I challenge the Cruelty to wake up the Justice

I challenge the War to wave the wings of liberty

I challenge the Devil to save Humanity

I challenge the vast world to conquer it
