They say that I have changed like its something bad!

Why do you look so grim? Why must you be so sad?

Life is a gift so seize the moment they say

What happened to you? Why do you keep everyone at bay?

Are you angry or mad or maybe just irritated by this and that?

Trust me no one will judge you here if you short, bald or fat.

But who would tell them that this person I have become is who I want to be

I have changed for a reason why don’t they see?

I used to be one of them and advice those all the silent peers I saw

Life is too short to be sad was the law

But now I see from their eyes

And feel in my heart that I can truly sympathize

My words hold more meaning then they used to back then

I was just a naive little kid who didn’t know what to do and when

Broke many hearts and got mine broken so oft

That I ended up with guilt so deep and eyes so soft

Now even if I look at someone in pain

It gives them more hope than those empty words would help them gain

Why do they shush me and look at me like I have gone mad?

I know i am different but that is because now I have felt that I never had.

It’s not sadness without a cause

It’s just that I have unlearned how to hide how painful it was

I don’t smile for others but act the way I want

How else would I have got rid of the memories that haunt?

Oh no don’t pity me for I am not the one suffering

Pity yourself who is hiding behind that false covering

Why must you lock your heart and paint that mask?

Why must you always walk that path and never let your heart ask?

Being human is not a crime

That is a truth you will learn to accept in time.
