Just say it isn’t so!

The stars say you have left me

So do the sun, the moon and the sea

The waves breaking on the shore say

You sit there all night and all day

With a guy who reeks of money

For goodness sake, honey

Just say it isn’t so!

That’s what the winds echo

As through the hills, they blow

And the flowers nod their heads in assent

Now, that you are gone, life is a punishment

The mountains seen to sigh

The rivers seen to cry

Just say it isn’t so!

The birds are singing sadly

The flowers are taking it badly

They just don’t dance by the lake

And it is all for my dead love’s sake

For you my heartless friend, my heart is pining

My dark cloud doesn’t even have a silver lining

Just say it isn’t so!

The sand screams and the sea-shells yell

My paradise has become an unbearable hell

The earth quakes and the skies quiver

The Oceans cry and the icy mountains shiver

My sun has set at mid-day

And one last time honey, I say

Just say it isn’t so!
