THE YATRA

I remain in silence
I remain in silence infront of the yatra
I see many others go here and there
I see many others go here and there but my luck is nowhere
I lay with my murmura
I lay with my murmura wondering about the beauty of the rich roaming
I question myself
I question myself what maģìç they have that I don’t
I dream for being the one
I dream for being the one to keep up my family and wish none
But I see none come towards me
But I see none come towards me but I do know the world is of none and god is with me
