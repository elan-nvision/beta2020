Nostalgia: something I remember when I was inside my mother's womb. Noices, pain still echoes in my ears, because I turned out to be a girl preparing to come into this world. 

        

BIRTH OF ANOTHER GIRL.

 Tears roll down my cheeks when ever I think of the maltreatment given to my mother when her husband (my father) came to know about my existence in her womb.

I wasn't there to witness such inhumanity she went through,

But my ears were developed.

I could here her heart beating fast as it was connected to mine,

I could here she begging my father to give me chance to live, 

I could feel the scars given to her every day and night.

I didn't see her body turning blue to purple, but I could feel her muscles bleeding and kicks given to her womb.

There were days when she wasn't given food to eat 

(Just a reminder, I wasn't there to witness stuff but I could feel her)

 

There were days when she wasn't given food to eat and water to drink,

But yet, she survived.To make me survive.

As the days passing by I realised it's time for me to come out and witness the abusive behaviour again.

But there came a moment when everything felt little different

My mind was distributed and travelled through varied forms of fear.

My instantly, a gentle touch absorbed all my uneasiness and made me open my eyes wide, for that I knew it the only person who could make me feel that was would be my mother 

But she was petrified, her eyes moving back and forth from our bed till the main door

Her lips were dried as if she was thirsty for love and affection.

Her trembling hands holding me close to her chest, assuring me that she'll never let me go.

But all of that disappeared when her eyes met mine, she gazed upon me with a smile with full joy and happiness as if it was a hustle journey of my birth 

Now I am 18 years old.

Stout,brave, enticing,exiquisite.

All by my mother's sacrifice

I can proudly say 

I AM A GIRL OF MY MOTHER'S WORLD.

As I grew up old I realised 

My dad was just another man who was against this sentence

BIRTH OF ANOTHER GIRL.
