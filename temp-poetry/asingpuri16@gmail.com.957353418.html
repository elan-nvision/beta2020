I hail you,
warriors and kings 

Hail you for all
the sorrows that you bring 

  

Hail you for the
misery 

You bestow upon
your family 

  

I hail you for
your strength 

That invites
death at every bend 

  

Hail, for you
are rich with 

Cursed
possessions and promises breached 

  

I hail you for
your power 

That hides your
entire deeds coward 

  

Hail you, for
what you yield 

Is at the mercy
of your metal shield 

  

I hail you,
warriors and kings Your fortune lets only poor receive its
poisonous sting
