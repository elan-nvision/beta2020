PERFECT.

You are Perfect

Perfect in every way

So perfect, I admire you

So impeccable, it is a delight to look at you

So pure, Your soul bears a depth i want to dive in

So flawless, I ruminate over my every sin

So simple, I fell in love with you

So innocent, it makes me anxious

It worries me

It terrifies me

That if I ever confess

and if we ever end up together

I might sully that perfect soul

Taint that beautiful mind

Disrupt the peace that lives inside you

With the darkness that lingers within me

With the darkness, no eyes can see

With the darkness that makes a monster out of me

So I rejected myself

Rejected me from the idea of what we could be

Because you are Perfect.
