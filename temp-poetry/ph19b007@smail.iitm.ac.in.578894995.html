Of romance with power

Wading through the darkness

Past trees bent over in salute

A man shudders into the cove

Of sinful absolute

He sets alight the torches

And settles upon cold stone

The embers crackle a gentle hum

And warm his frigid bones

With the fall of night he comes

With the grin of dawn he's gone

But his evil mistress lurks on

In her he finds his solace

When the light begins to fade

He feeds upon her malice

To satisfy he hunger for pain

Throughout dusk he shelters

In her malevolent embrace

For her sinister grace

He welcomes the wicked deeds

She wishes for him to do

For until daylight cracks

he is hers to rule

He is but a broken man

Shattered from within

His eyes show no emotion

But his grief

Urges him to sin

He is just for her a tool

An instrument for destruction

A canal to channel 

Her vengeful annihilations

His weakening frame withers

Insanity a repreive

But yet he holds on

To betray his mistress, He cannot concieve 

Reduced to just a vessel

To hold the mistress's animus

A husk charred from disease

A shell that has grown gangrenous

Until at last

The vessel cracks

Screams of agony

Render the scene black

The mistress seethes in rage

Her gnarled fingers claw his back

He turns around to face

The grotesque reality he had

She emanates pure evil

Her intentions just to hurt

To obliterate all meaning

And smile in the dust

Her eyes glint with spite

Tainted with years of rust

Hope leaks out of his wounds

In vain he tries to run

He crumbles on to the floor

He knows he is done

A last struggle to escape

He comes full circle

She reaches out for his throat

On his own hands he chokes
