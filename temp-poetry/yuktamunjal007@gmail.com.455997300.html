Beauty 

Title- Petrichor

Typical mundane evening

I looked at the sky

Changing its colour

Preparing to welcome the dark grey clouds

Clouds, waiting for the thunder to roll

Thunder, demanding the rain to pour

And it starts ,

Pouring heavily

First rain of the season

Incessant and fierce

Clouds giving away all they have

I stood there , blank

Hesitated a bit and shouted

"Why does it have to rain today ?"

Grumbling and whining again

I saw a kid

Gleeful and gay

Dancing to the sounds of rain

As if nothing to fear , nothing to lose

Witnessed the petrichor of the first rain

Warm and pleasant smell

Filling me with joy

Lightening up the mood

I let go of the hesitation

Stepped forward

Petrichor exuding from the soil

Liberating me from all the boundaries ,

I set for myself

Stress unwinding

Chains opening

I felt alive , free , whole

Memories gushed in

I could see my childhood in that boy

My younger self dancing to the tunes of life

Tapping on the melody of placid waters

Away from the fast pace metro life ,

Which changes everything in the spur of a moment

Changed me too

I wish I could go back

become the same carefree bird

I wish I could go home
