SCARS

 Its been a long time now,

And time heals wounds they say,

There are scars all over me ,

And I’ll just let them stay.


Wounds heal. Marks stay.

At least those who mark a silent prey.

Burning scars see revenge some say,

But in remission there’s peace, there’s grey.


The scars hurt this morning

They do all the time.

And I’ll let them burn,

For burning scars are beautiful and benign.
