A letter to my 14 year old self...

Dear me,

             I know it's confusing. The world is not the same. The people around you are'nt behaving the same as they used to.

           You are gonna be 14 years old in 5 minutes. I'm here to tell you a few things which are probably the last things you wanna know today.

             Did someone praise you for something amazing you just did? They must have, because you were always amazing. You love them, don't you? You wanna spend more time with these people because you adore them. Name these people type 1.

             Let's about type 2. Did someone insult and humiliate you by pointing out every small mistake you've done? I am sure someone did. And trust me when I say this - Type 1 and Type 2 are the same. The difference is in the time. Type 2 is basically the future of type 1. They've found that insignificant mistake of yours.

           Run. Run far away from these people. They are the one who's jealous and you intimidate them. Don't get depressed and spend sleepless nights thinking how people changed overnight.YOU DON'T DESERVE THEM.

             And did someone just defend you in public and corrected you in private by pointing out your mistake only to you? Well, I'm sure you hate them because they are'nt in your agreement. But eventually these are the ones who truly support you and care about you. They go hard on you sometimes to set you in the right path. What do you call these type of people? Type 3? No...

            YOUR PARENTS DUMMY!!! PARENTS!

              They want you to do the right thing and you don't realize that. Believe me, you're still an amateur! Hold their hands and hug them tight. Thank them for everything. They just want you to do your best. They are the ones whom you should love.

              Last but not the least. F.R.I.E.N.D.S. . I know it's a great comic series but I'm talking about YOUR friends. You love your friends,right? How many of them supported you when you're depressed? Everyone? Certainly not. They are just like type 1 , 2 and parents. Acknowledge the ones who like the way you are, by character and personality. The ones who support you in every line of life. It's difficult, but it's totally worth it.

              Surround yourself with true people and abandon the fake. Don't waste your time and energy on useless stuff and people.

HAPPY BIRTHDAY CHAMP!!! Do what you love, you'll love it more. Work hard on your dreams and ambitions from now itself. See ya again if I get a chance.

BYE!!

                                                                                                                                                                                          -Future you.
