- A WAITING ON AISLE -

So sad that you left me,left me in a white gown, with a bouquet in hand,

A veil on my head and hopes in my blood

Petals kept falling,the night was arriving but

you never came

Were you afraid?

So sad that you left me waiting,waiting on the aisle

So sad that I want to tell and hear you but all i can do is wonder.

I see the thorns below the petals

I fly away, I fly away

This time to meet me and be better.
