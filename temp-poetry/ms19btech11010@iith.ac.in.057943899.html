THE GUILTY TIME

Time’s crumbled to death

With the curse of losing a true love

Gravely punished

For allowing the memories to be lost

Sternly beaten

For letting the tragedy to happen

Inhumanely hanged

For permitting the situations to turn upside down

Deeply bruised

For invoking the dreams of a felicitous future

Severely burnt

For knotting the ties it knew couldn’t stand long

(pain & suffering)
