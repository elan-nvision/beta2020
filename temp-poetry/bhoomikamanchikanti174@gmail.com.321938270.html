- BREAKTHROUGH THE CAGE OF PATRIARCHY -


Don't drag me into your cage.

I don't want to be hurt and left in a cage.

Don't bring the bars of this cage closer to trap better.

I love myself

I want to protect myself so,

Let me out! Free as I can be!

Don't hold too tight.

Don't paint blood.

Don't be the reason for a loss.

In this world filled with chaos,

let my voice be heard and let me shine.

No curse can break my vision

when I'm building the 'me' I envisioned.
