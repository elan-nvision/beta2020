For a new
beginning again 

The universe
calls out again, again 

  

As the storm
rose up to the sky 

Darkness
suddenly stretched its legs 

The dusty clouds
covered the heights and the land 

The day became
dark like nights 

Nights even
darker 

Fear of the
turmoil of the nights 

Surrounded all
people, all matter 

But the warm
smile of the sun 

Peaked from the
eastern sky again 

  

For a new
beginning again 

The universe
calls out again, again 

  

The storm was so
strong 

It could make
huge mountains tremble 

The storm was so
strong 

It could uproot
trees 

Oh! What may
have happened to the shelter of twigs! 

When rock
palaces shook 

Tell me, the
bird of hope, where were you hiding? 

That you went up
to the sky 

Head held high
with pride again 

  

For a new beginning
again 

The universe
calls out again, again 

  

Between the
canines of the biting sky 

The dawn smiles 

The sky screams,
the sky thunders 

And the flight
of birds flies singing birdsongs for miles 

See that bird
soaring with a twig in her beak 

So easily, so pleasantly
she zooms past the winds of creek 

The sorrow of
destruction can never surpass 

The joys of
construction 

Among the
silence of apocalypse the creation sings again 

  

For a new
beginning again The universe calls out again, again
