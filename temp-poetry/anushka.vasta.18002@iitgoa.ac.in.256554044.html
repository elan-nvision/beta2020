SADNESS

-Anushka Vatsa

  

Strolling down the meadow a soul 

beneath the lavender sheet

with great red tinge I saw her troll, 

sky colours disclosed emotions in fleet. 

For the nature could be the one 

with tree twigs wide open 

longing to console 

the depths broken. 

  

The mountains echoed the veracity, 

the rain drizzling bouts 

Gentle wind very cleverly 

tousled a few strands over the visage 

Letting the realisation brace 

that truth never really hits you,

it smacks you in the face. 

  

The world shrieked torment on 

and came paving way down it chuckled 

For all we knew when it stormed upon 

Time could no longer be knuckled  

  

And before can thou really tell 

The gushes did the job 

For there never really is a hell 

than wailing behind the mob 

  

And just like that, the stars, 

without any rush of affection 

dawned and the pink sky 

turned dark reciting scars. 

And the ever sparkling moon  

now wept unhidden from thy. 

-Anushka Vatsa, IIT Goa
