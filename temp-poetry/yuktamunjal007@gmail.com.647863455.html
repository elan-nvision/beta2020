Suffering

Title - How do you live with yourself ? 

People ask me, " How do you live with yourself ? "

The exact same question I have been asking myself for the past decade

How do I live with myself ?

I answer that , I don't

I don't live with myself

My repentance does , My shame does , My guilt does

But I , don't

I am still back there

In that six by eight feet dark and dingy cell

Darkness so profound

I lost myself in it

I am still in those white walls which became so familiar

Eventually they turned pale and so did I

I am still with that little window

Through which entered the sunshine of hope

I am still with those metal bars

Which trapped me , my felicity , my soul

I left that familiar cell

Entered the unfamiliar but real world

Those memories still haunt me

Maybe because Memories are like a boomerang

Doesn't matter how much you let go

Eventually they will come back to you

I was so used to the colour black and white

Abundance of colour outside , perplexed me

Now hundreds of people around me

So many eyes but asking the exact same question

I have been asking myself for the past decade

How do you live with yourself ?

In your eyes I commit the same crime everyday

The same crime everyday that I committed ten years back

I am still that person I was ten years back

Why ?

Maybe because criminals don't get second chances

Maybe because once a criminal will always be a criminal

But I stand in my room

In front of the mirror and see the difference

The difference in my eyes

Once filled with greed but now filled with guilt

The difference in my hands

Once blood stained but now folded , asking for forgiveness

I know I can never take back what I did

But forgive me god

I was merely a kid
